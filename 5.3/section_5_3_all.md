## Problem 75

**Type:** Exponential Equation  

### Given

> Solve 3^(x^2-7)=27^(2x).

### Use common base

27^(2x)=(3^3)^(2x)=3^(6x). So x^2-7=6x.

### Solve quadratic

x^2-6x-7=0 -> (x-7)(x+1)=0

### Final Answer

> x=7 or x=-1.

## Problem 77
