$$\text{Numerator} = (x - 2)·(x^2 + 2x + 4)$$
$$\text{Denominator} = (x - 3)·(x - 2)$$

  x = 2

$$ H(x) = (x^2 + 2x + 4)/(x - 3) $$

  x = 3

  x = 2 (hole at (2, -12))

### Step 4 - Horizontal or Oblique Asymptote

Rule used: numerator degree is exactly 1 more, so there is a slant (oblique) asymptote from polynomial division.

No horizontal asymptote.
Oblique asymptote: y = x + 5

### Final Answer

> Vertical asymptotes: x = 3 | Horizontal asymptote: None | Oblique asymptote: y = x + 5 | Holes: (2, -12)
