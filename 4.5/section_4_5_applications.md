## Problem 58

**Type:** Rational Application  

### Given

> P(t) = 50(1 + 0.5t)/(2 + 0.01t)

### Step 1 - Write the Model

Population model:
$$ P(t) = 2500*(t + 2)/(t + 200) $$
where t is measured in months.

### Step 2 - Part (a): Population at t = 0

Substitute t = 0:
$$ P(0) = 25 $$
So the initial population is 25.

### Step 3 - Part (b): Population after 5 months

Substitute t = 5:
$$ P(5) = 3500/41 $$
So after 5 months, population is 3500/41.

### Step 4 - Part (c): Horizontal Asymptote and Meaning

Because numerator and denominator are both degree 1,
the horizontal asymptote is ratio of leading coefficients:
$$ y = 2500 $$
Interpretation: the population approaches 2500 insects as time becomes very large.

### Final Answer

> (a) P(0) = 25 | (b) P(5) = 3500/41 | (c) Horizontal asymptote y = 2500; sustainable largest population ≈ 2500
